About the Archive

Preserving Contemporary Art History

Founded in 2010, our archive is dedicated to documenting and preserving significant works from emerging and established contemporary artists. Our collection spans multiple mediums, with a focus on works that explore themes of memory, identity, and material culture.

This exhibition showcases a selection from our permanent collection, featuring 35 artists from 15 different countries. The works on display represent a decade of acquisitions and highlight the archive's commitment to supporting artistic innovation.

Current Exhibition

Fragments of Memory: A Retrospective

This exhibition brings together works from the archive's permanent collection that explore the theme of memory through various media. The selected pieces represent a decade of collecting and showcase evolving approaches to documenting personal and collective memories.
